The Best Movies According to Alexis

My top 3 movies of all-time.

What Happened to Monday

Provides a gripping and dystopian view of a future where overpopulation and strict family-size regulations lead to dire consequences, making it a standout film for its intense storyline and thrilling twists.

Interstellar

Masterfully combines stunning visuals, a deeply emotional narrative, and scientifically grounded exploration themes that push the boundaries of space, time, and human potential.

Ex Machina

Really interesting sci-fi movie that has a plot that seems eerily realistic and thought-provoking, delving into the implications of artificial intelligence and its impact on human identity and ethics.
